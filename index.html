These are some of visualiztions I created with d3 as a side project.

Roger Federer's Air Travel in 2014

Tennis players travel around the world all year round to play in tournaments. Using d3 geo projections and topojson data, I marked cities and flight paths that Roger Federer flew in 2014.

Data was scraped from www.atptour.com wtih 'beautifulsoup' python library, then parsed and cleaned.

World Immunization Trend - line graph

Immunization is an important mark in modern medicine. I wanted to visualize the trend of immunization in countries over the years. I first began with a line graph in conjunction with scatter plot. You can select a country to view its immunization history from 1980 to 2013.

*Data Source: wwww.oecd.org

- View Immunization Line Graph

World Immunization Trend - stacked bar graph

After creating the line graph of immunization history, I wanted to create a visualiation that allowed easier comparison between countries. The line graph made it diffcult comparisons among countires. Stacked bar graph seemed like a rational choice (and I just wanted to learn how to make one in d3). I used distinct hue for each disease rather than varying saturation and value in order to visually separate each disease.
