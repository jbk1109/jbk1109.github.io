These are some of visualiztions I created with d3 as a side project.

Tennis players travel around the world all year round to play in tournaments. On the map, I marked cities and flight paths that Roger Federer flew in 2014.
Federer

Immunization is an important mark in modern medicine. I wanted to visualize the trend of immunization in countries over the years. It's a simple line graph where you can select a country to view its immunization history.
Immunization

After creating the line graph of immunization history, I wanted to create a visualiation that allowed easier comparison between countries. Stacked bar graph seemed like a rational choice (and I just wanted to learn how to make one in d3).
Immunization Stacked Bar
